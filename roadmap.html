Public roadmap

What exists now, what comes next

This roadmap is grounded in the active desktop build, the repo’s planning documents, and the current office deployment direction. It distinguishes what already works from what is still a forward-looking concept.

Now

Working today

  • Standard User and Supervisor Windows builds.
  • Sticky-note overlays with timers, transparency, themes, and logging.
  • Network file handoff notifications and routed open actions.
  • Supervisor dashboard foundation and role-aware desktop UI.
  • Website-based update origin and direct role downloads.
Near term

In active refinement

  • More polished public website and public materials.
  • Stronger notification behavior, stacking, and dismissal consistency.
  • Smoother sticky-note sending flows and peer management UX.
  • Richer dashboard visibility for deadlines, acknowledgements, and cleanup prompts.
Next layer

Messaging and operational overlays

The next product tier is not just more notifications. It is a more expressive overlay communication layer: task notes, response notes, lightweight team messaging, and better dashboard aggregation.

  • Single-send post-it messages.
  • Persistent chat overlays.
  • Group announcements for coordinators and supervisors.
  • Stronger file lifecycle and cleanup prompts.
Longer horizon

Blue AI and company knowledge systems

The future-facing plan is a company-aware assistant grounded in projects, policies, and approved knowledge bases. This is explicitly a governed layer, not an invisible watcher process.

  • Project-grounded retrieval and answering.
  • Policy-aware assistant behavior.
  • Server health and coordination support for administrators.
  • Controlled cloud broadcast for weather, alerts, and org-wide notices.
